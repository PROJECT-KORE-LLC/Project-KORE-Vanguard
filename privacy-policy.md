Privacy Policy for Project Kore LLC 

1. The Prime Directive (Zero Data Collection)
Amber Tucker LLC does not collect, store, or "phone home" any personal data. Our tools—including The Archive and Moon’s Hearth—are built as Local-First Single Page Applications (SPAs). Your data lives on your device. Period.

2. No Telemetry, No Ghosts
We have stripped our code of all trackers, cookies, and third-party analytics. There are no "energy vampires" watching how you use the vault. This is a core requirement of our 0.6% performance floor.

3. Third-Party Hosting
While the tools themselves are private, they are hosted via [GitHub Pages / Vercel]. These platforms may log basic technical metadata (like IP addresses) for security and delivery, but this data never reaches the LLC and is never sold.

4. Somatic Integrity
Any audio or visual assets used for somatic regulation are loaded locally. We do not track your focus sessions or your "breath" cycles.

5. Your Sovereignty
Because we do not have your data, we cannot sell it, lose it, or give it away. You are the sole administrator of your vault.
